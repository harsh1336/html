Paragraph writing involves composing a unified group of sentences about a single main idea, typically structured with a topic sentence, supporting sentences that provide details and evidence, and a concluding sentence to summarize or transition. A well-written paragraph is organized, coherent, and contributes to the clarity and flow of a larger text like an essay or report.
